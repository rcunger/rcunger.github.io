I'm a PhD student at Princeton University, advised by Mihalis Dafermos. I'm interested in general relativity, nonlinear wave equations, and scalar curvature geometry.

In Fall 2022/Spring 2023, I will be visiting Mihalis Dafermos at Cambridge University.

I received my undergrad degree from the University of Tennessee, where I was mentored by Alexandre Freire.
